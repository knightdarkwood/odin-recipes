Omurice

Description

Follow this recipe to learn how to create the perfect Omurice at home using these simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 2 Hotdogs
  • Milk
  • Soy Sauce
  • Ketchup
  • 1 & 1/2 Cup Rice

Steps

  1. Crack and whisk the eggs in a bowl.
  2. Add a dash of milk and soy sauce.
  3. Mix well then cook in the airfryer for 9 minutes at 150 degrees celcius.
  4. Cook the rice and let it cool.
  5. Mix the ketchup and soy sauce with the rice and cook in the airfryer for 20 minutes at 155 degrees celcius.
  6. Serve together with ketchup topping.
