Description

Follow this recipe to learn how to create the perfect Japanese Curry at home using these simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2x Potato
  • 2x Carrot
  • 1x Onion
  • 1x Block of Curry Roux
  • 1kg Chicken Breast
  • Japanese Breading
  • 2 Tablespoons Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons Ketchup
  • 2 Tablespoons Honey
  • 4 Cups Water

Steps

  1. Chop the chicken breast into thin cuts and marinate for 20 minutes.
  2. Cover the chicken in breading and cook in the air fryer for 15 minutes at 180 degrees celcius.
  3. Chop the vegetables and boil in a pot until fully cooked.
  4. Add the soy souce, ketchup and honey.
  5. Add the curry roux, bloil and stir until sauce is smooth.
  6. Let it cool down and serve with freshly cooked rice.
