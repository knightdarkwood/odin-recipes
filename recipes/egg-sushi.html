Egg Sushi

Home

Image by Andrew M. Annuar via Flickr

Description

Follow this recipe to learn how to create the perfect Egg Sushi at home using these simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 1 Cup Japanese Rice
  • 1/4 Cup White Vinegar
  • 1/8 Cup White Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Seaweed
  • Kewpie

Steps

  1. Crack and whisk the eggs in a bowl.
  2. Add a dash of soy sauce and sugar.
  3. Mix well then cook in the airfryer for 11 minutes at 150 degrees celcius.
  4. Cook the rice and let it cool.
  5. Mix the vinegar and sugar with the rice.
  6. Cut the egg and assemble the sushi.
